Part II: Description

This chart shows the data of the windspeed, PM2.5, and temperature. By clicking on the labels, you can isolate each type of data and compare them. For example,
choose the PM2.5 and the Windspeed, or PM2.5 and the temperature. This comparison of climatic conditions can generally see some trends and influence.
However, different types of variable have different units, I added the reference line to help comparsion.

Findings

It can be seen that PM2.5 will decrease when the wind speed is high. Because of the heating supply reason, pm2.5 will be higher when the temperatures goes lower.
